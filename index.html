About Me

Bio

Hello! My name is Grace Reiland, and my preferred pronouns are she/her. I live about twenty minutes south of Pitt, and I chose to go to school here because I have always loved Pittsburgh. I am a very big cat, plant, and people person!

Goals

Although I am pursuing a career in Psychology, I believe this can be generalized to a more general topic of people overall. I'm interested in how people perceive, behave, and relate to one another. Studying psychology gives me the potential to create change.
